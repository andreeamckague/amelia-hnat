WHAT IS AN ART COMMISISON?

A unique experience where a client pays an artist to create a custom work of art. Typically, these are investments made for personal collections or deeply personal gifts.

1

Establish a vision and purpose for your custom art piece.

2

Define key details like medium, theme, style, size, and budget.

3

Reach out to Amelia with your details and any questions.

4

Accept her fair offer and be patient during the process.
